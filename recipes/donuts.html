Sugared Campfire Donuts

Description

Turn refrigerated biscuits into delicious, sugary donuts.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup White Sugar
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 10 oz Biscuit Dough
  • 3 Tablespoons melted Butter

Steps

  1. Toss together the sugar and cinnamon in a bowl, set aside.
  2. Cut each biscuit into thirds, and roll into balls.
  3. Skewer on a toasting fork, and cook over hot coals, turning constantly, until golden brown.
  4. Dip into melted butter, then roll in cinnamon sugar.
